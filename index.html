What We Aim To Do

This advocacy aims to raise awareness about the importance of sharks in our marine wildlife and encourage others to strive to protect them more.

Why Sharks Need Our Help

Sharks face so many threats. Including overfishing, destruction of habitat, and the most popular, yet saddest one, Shark Finning. Shark finning is the process of man purposefully catching sharks, slicing off their fins, and simply throwing back their bodies, leaving them to die. They need our help to stop these threats from happening.

How You Can Help

By understanding their role and supporting more advocacies, we can help save these incredible, outstanding species and ensure that our oceans will remain healthy and balanced for the upcoming generations of new marine wildlife species.
